Our Project

Our project is to support vulnerable homeless family in our community. We provide food, shelter, and clothing to the homeless family in our community. We also provide them with job training and job placement. We also provide them with mental health counseling and other support services. We believe that everyone deserves a chance to succeed and we are committed to helping homeless family in our community get back on their feet.
